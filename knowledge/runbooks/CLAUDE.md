# Runbooks

@../CLAUDE.md

Operational how-tos and procedures for recurring processes.

## Conventions

- Each runbook: purpose, prerequisites, step-by-step, troubleshooting, related links
- Runbooks describe *how* to execute a process, not *what* was decided (that's decisions/)
- Keep steps actionable and sequenced -- a new team member should be able to follow them
- Cross-reference templates in `analysis/_templates/` where applicable
